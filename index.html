What is Hacker House Thailand?

Hacker House Thailand is a co-living space for developers, startup founders, and designers etc. At this house, tenants not only live together in an environment that is designed specfically to fit their busy life style but also code, learn, and network with other like-minded professionals in Thailand, SEA, as well as in Silicon Valley. Equipped with the most advanced smart home technologies, we maximize and ensure your safety, comfort, and convenient while minimize the total household energy consumption. We offer free high speed WiFi, free room cleaning service, free shared bike, free coffee and tea, as well as free access to the World best online classes and trainings. This house is located in a prime location that is close to markets, street vendors, restaurants, shopping malls, and within walking distance  to major public transportations. We understand what you want because we are also developers :).
